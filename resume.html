About Me

What I do: Build bridges, not walls. I create inclusive, empowering spaces for LGBTQIA+ youth through mentorship, creative advocacy, and trauma-informed care.

Why I do it: Because it's not about 'you' or 'me'—it's about 'us.' My work is driven by a deep commitment to justice, community, and the belief that no young person should feel invisible or unheard.

How I do it: Together, or not at all. I lead collaborative projects that use technology and art as tools for empowerment, always centering the voices and agency of the communities I serve.

Professional Highlights

  • Project Lead, Starlight Brigade Council – Creative leadership focused on digital inclusion and queer visibility.
  • Peer Mentor, America On Tech – Mentored youth in tech and web development through trauma-informed support.
  • Career & Academic Coach, NMIC – Provided career and academic guidance to at-risk youth.

Portfolio & Projects

  • The Signal Test & Lyra Project – An investigative art piece on algorithmic bias and queer erasure.
  • The Forgotten Library – A curated collection of interactive stories and digital artifacts.
