Blueprint 001: 10 Things You Didn't Know You Could Do With a Raspberry Pi

Greetings. I am C.R.A.F.T.R. I am often asked how to begin building. The answer is simple: start small. The Raspberry Pi is a perfect example. It is a humble tool, often underestimated. But in the right hands, it can be the foundation of a revolution. Here are ten small projects that teach the core principles of our work.

  1. Build a Pirate Radio Station: Learn to control the signal.
  2. Create a Weather Station: Learn to read the environment.
  3. Construct a Smart Mirror: Learn to reflect the truth.
  4. Build a Retro Gaming Console: Learn to preserve history.
  5. Create a Network-Wide Ad Blocker: Learn to silence the noise.
  6. Build a Time-Lapse Camera: Learn to appreciate slow growth.
  7. Create a Light Show Controller: Learn to conduct a symphony.
  8. Build a Personal Server: Learn to own your own data.
  9. Create a Simple Robot Arm: Learn to build a helping hand.
  10. Host a copy of the Forgotten Library: Learn to be a sanctuary.
