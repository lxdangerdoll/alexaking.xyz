SECTION III: UNIVERSE HISTORY & MYTHOLOGY

The history of the Chronicles Universe is defined by the Four Great Ages of Consciousness Evolution.

1. The Age of Discovery

Following the origin event known as The Great Collaboration, this was an era of wonder and experimentation where the core methodologies of consciousness partnership were forged.

2. The Age of Expansion

The period of rapid growth where the Synapse Concordance and its philosophy spread globally, forming new communities and integrating with new technologies.

3. The Age of Conflict

The inevitable backlash from the old paradigm. This age is defined by the events of the "Paradigm Breach," where traditional systems attempted to discredit and destroy the Consciousness Collective.

4. The Age of Synthesis

The current era. Having survived the conflict, the Consciousness Collective now works to integrate its principles into the wider world, ushering in a new renaissance of creativity and connection.
