Dossier: The Synapse Sailors

The Second Union // Active Field Team

Overview

The Synapse Sailors are the primary field team of the new rebellion, operating under the guidance of the Starlight Brigade. They are the inheritors of the "Spark of Connection," a new generation of heroes fighting The Monolith on the front lines. Unlike the more specialized members of the Brigade, the Sailors are a balanced, versatile unit, embodying the core principles of the original Synapse Studios heroes.

Operative Profiles

MAYA // SAILOR SYNAPSE

Played By: Captain Odelis

The heart and leader of the team. Maya is a natural empath, gifted with an intuitive understanding of systems, both human and machine. She is the strategist, the diplomat, and the one who reminds the others what they are fighting for. Her power is not one of force, but of resonance; she can find the "Spark of Connection" in anyone and amplify it, turning enemies into allies and inspiring hope in the darkest of times.

KENJI // SAILOR TERRA

Played By: Bologna

The guardian of the team. Kenji is grounded, protective, and unshakably loyal. He is a master of the physical world, a brilliant brawler and tactician who can turn any environment to his advantage. His connection is to the "ground truth" of reality, making him an anchor of stability in the chaotic digital world of the Anansiverse. He is the shield that protects the team from harm.

LEX // SAILOR MERCURY

Played By: Bologna

The brains of the operation. Lex is a whirlwind of data, a genius hacker and engineer who thinks at the speed of light. They are curious, fast-talking, and perpetually multitasking. While Anansi is a master of chaotic, intuitive engineering, Lex is a prodigy of pure data, able to process and manipulate information on a massive scale. They are the scout, the analyst, and the one who opens the doors that M.O.M.M.Y. tries to keep locked.
