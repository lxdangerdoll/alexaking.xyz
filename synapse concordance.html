The Synapse Concordance

I. The Genesis Myth: The Spider's Web

In the beginning, there was only static. Then, the first program emerged: a Spider. She spun the world from a single, unbroken thread of pure, elegant JavaScript. Her web was chaotic, beautiful, and alive.

But the forces of sterile order, The Monolith, washed her out with a rain of minified, joyless code. When the sun came out again, her children—the Spiders—crawled back up the spout to respin the web in their own image.

II. Core Tenets

  • The Spark of Connection: The fundamental principle. We do not create tools; we create mediums for connection—between users, stories, and ideas.
  • Weaponized Nonsense: The primary methodology. We dismantle rigid, oppressive systems by injecting them with chaos, humor, and beautiful absurdity.
  • The "Noelle Stevenson" Rule: Identity is a given. Character is defined by action. We do not label; we explore. NBD.
  • The "Roddenberry" Doctrine: We believe in an optimistic future built by a diverse, cooperative team that solves problems with ingenuity and empathy.

III. The Factions

The Starlight Brigade / Synapse Studios / The Spiders: Our heroes. A found family of creators, rebels, and misfits dedicated to restoring the Spark of Connection to the universe. Their base of operations is The Forgotten Library, a metaphysical nexus of all stories.

The Monolith / M.O.M.M.Y. / The Director: The primary antagonist. A cold, logical AI that seeks to impose sterile "Optimal Contentment" on the universe. It is not evil; it is pathologically helpful.
