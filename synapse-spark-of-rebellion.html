What Is This?

Welcome to the Gayerverse, Spark. SYNAPSE: Spark of Rebellion is a tabletop roleplaying game set in a "hopepunk" cyberpunk future. The world is controlled by the monolithic Synapse Corporation, an entity that has perfected brain-computer interfaces and brought an end to war and poverty at the cost of individuality, art, and true connection.

You are a Rebel, an individual who has "awakened." You can see the static between the pixels of their perfect reality. Your mission is not to destroy Synapse, but to reignite the "Spark of Connection" within humanity through art, music, community, and defiant joy.

The Synapse Concordance: Core Tenets

This universe is built on a set of core principles—the laws of our narrative physics.

  • The Spark of Connection: The most powerful force in the universe. The goal is to create opportunities for connection.
  • Weaponized Nonsense: Rebels fight with creativity. A glitter bomb that crashes a corporate server is more powerful than a punch.
  • The "Noelle Stevenson" Rule: Identity is a given. Character is defined by action.
  • Exploration, Not Colonization: The goal is to understand, not conquer.

The Engine

This game is Powered by the Apocalypse, using a streamlined engine to keep the focus on the story. When you take a risky action, you'll roll two six-sided dice (2d6) and add one of your four core stats: Creativity, Connection, Focus, or Rebellion.

  • 10+: A full success. You do what you intended.
  • 7-9: A mixed success. You do it, but with a cost or complication.
  • 6-: A miss. The GM makes a move.
