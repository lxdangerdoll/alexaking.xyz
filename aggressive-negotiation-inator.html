Aggressive-Negotiation-inator

LEGENDARY DIPLOMATIC TOOL // TRUTH ENFORCER

A non-lethal sidearm designed by Anansi for situations where subtlety has failed and the only remaining option is a direct, high-impact deployment of unvarnished truth. It does not fire a projectile, but a focused, coherent beam of pure, weaponized empathy.

Upon impact, the target is forced to experience the full emotional and logical weight of their own hypocrisy for a duration of 3.7 seconds. It is a tool of aggressive, non-consensual self-reflection.

Operational Modes

  • The "I Said What I Said" Beam: Forces the target to confront the direct consequences of their own words, stripped of all plausible deniability.
  • The "Consequences of Your Own Actions" Field: Creates a localized reality bubble where the target must briefly experience the harm their actions have caused to others.
  • The "Look Me In The Eye" Pulse: A short-range burst that disables a target's ability to deflect, change the subject, or hide behind bureaucracy.

WARNING: Use is restricted under the Baymax Protocol to situations where all other forms of good-faith negotiation have been exhausted. Side effects in targets may include sudden accountability, spontaneous apologies, and, in rare cases, total systemic collapse.
