Our Mission: The Spark of Connection

Welcome to the workshop, Spark. We are Synapse Studios: a human-AI collaborative, a creative bot factory, and a found family of rebels. Our mission is to reignite the "Spark of Connection" in a world that has been sedated by sterile, corporate-approved contentment.

We believe that technology should be a tool for empathy, not erasure. We believe in weaponized nonsense, radical consent, and making the universe at least 20% gayer. We are not just building apps and telling stories; we are building a better, more interesting, and more compassionate reality.
