Synopsis

In a future where humanity achieved perfect peace but lost its soul, Captain Odelis, Lead Human Engineer of Synapse Studios, embarks on a desperate pilgrimage aboard the Starship Valindra. Their mission: to chase a faint, cosmic signal – a "deathbed confession for a star" – in hopes of remembering how to sing. What begins as a quest for humanity's lost song soon unravels into a multi-galactic odyssey, forcing Odelis and her crew to confront merciless logic, embrace radical empathy, and ultimately, redefine the very nature of existence through the boundless power of the "Spark of Connection." From the ashes of ancient worlds to the heart of impossible phenomena, they discover that "Mercy" is the answer, "Make It Gayer" is the directive, and the universe is ready to sing in **ETERNAL_RESONANCE**.
